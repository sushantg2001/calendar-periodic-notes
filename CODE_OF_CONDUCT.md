# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainers through a GitHub issue or private contact. Maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
